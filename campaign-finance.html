Campaign Finance – Constitutional Amendment

Why do we need to amend the Consitution for campaign finance regulation?
Due to Citizen’s United, our campaign finance systems are substantially unregulated. This has empowered the voices of a select few, allowed untraceable advertisements to circulate, and corroded the ability of the people to govern themselves. This bill calls on our Federal Congress to propose an amendment to the Constitution to overturn the Citizen’s United decision, so States may regulate campaign finance systems. If a bill is not amendment is not proposed within 6 months, Massachusetts will be the 6th state to call for a Constitutional Convention to propose the amendment.
